### What to test in EVERY unit
1. **Sound:** Knock on the wall adjacent to neighbor unit. Listen for echo/transmission.
2. **Smell:** Pet smell, cigarette smell, mold smell, weed smell. Hard to eliminate.
3. **Light:** What time is it? What direction does sun come in? Will it be like this at 6am?
4. **HVAC:** Turn on AC. Listen for grinding. Feel airflow.
5. **Water:** Turn on shower. Feel pressure + temperature stability.
6. **Floor:** Bounce on flooring. Listen for creaks (subfloor issues).
7. **Windows:** Open them. Check window seal + screen condition.
8. **View:** Take a panoramic photo. Lookup blocked-view risk later.
9. **Building cell signal:** Are you getting 4 bars or 1 bar on your phone inside?
10. **Elevator timing:** Wait one cycle to see actual wait time at this hour.
