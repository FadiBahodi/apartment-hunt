The flow

  1. Filter + sort the list (CVH ≤15min, ≤$2,000, etc — multi-select).
  2. Click a building → detail panel opens with photos, cost breakdown, reviews, fit score.
  3. Heart it (❤️) to save to favorites. Add to compare (⚖️) to stack 2-4 side-by-side.
  4. Mark called/toured as you progress — saved locally so you don't lose track.
  5. 📅 Timeline shows the week-by-week May 11 → July 1 plan (51 days).
  6. Notes per building — type your own observations; saved to localStorage.

Critical reminders

  • Run Google Maps at your actual shift times (5:15am, 7am, 1pm, 3:30pm, 11pm, 7am post-night). My drive times are priors, not posteriors.
  • Condo rentals: demand the condo declaration before signing. RTA s.14 doesn't override pet bans in the declaration.
  • Tour at 11pm and 7am, not midday — different building entirely.
  • Avoid list (sidebar) is documented from CBC/Bed Bug Registry/listings — those buildings have real problems.
